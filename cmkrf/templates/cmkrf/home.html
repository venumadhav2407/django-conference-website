About Us

The Indian Institute of Surgeon Summit is a prestigious global gathering of distinguished surgeons, medical experts, and healthcare innovators. This premier event fosters collaboration, showcases pioneering research, and explores the latest advancements in surgical techniques and medical technology.

More than just a conference, the summit is a convergence of minds dedicated to pushing the boundaries of modern surgery. Attendees gain unparalleled opportunities to engage with leading professionals through keynote addresses, insightful panel discussions, and hands-on workshops.

Beyond academic and technical discourse, the summit nurtures a spirit of mentorship and camaraderie, bringing together experienced surgeons and young professionals to shape the future of surgical excellence.
